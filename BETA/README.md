## install_new_hotkeys.sh
	- repeat-on--hold key presses
	- Fn or R3 (right joystick press) both act as the hotkey
	- HOTKEY + D-pad up or down adjusts brightness
	- HOTKEY + D-pad left or right adjusts volume
	- HOTKEY + L or R triggers adjust volume
	- HOTKEY + L2 or R2 triggers adjust brightness

## fix_Retrorun.sh
- repairs Retrorun and Retrorun32 controls for r45h and r36h consoles
- repairs missing audio in Retrorun32 on all consoles
